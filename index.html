From April to July 2015 I completed General Assembly's Web Development Immersive Course, during which I coded all day, every day for twelve weeks.


The course was full-stack, full-time, with a syllabus weighted towards developing a proficiency in Javascript (including jQuery, Ajax, Backbone), HTML, CSS and Ruby on Rails.
